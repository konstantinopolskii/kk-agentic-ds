Wealthy pipeline preview

Eight states of one document. Seven operator stages plus the client view. Every state lives in its own file under this folder. Open any to see how the doc accumulates as the operator walks brief to delivered.

The shell stays. Three columns, only the middle scrolls. The book column dominates; the inspector compresses. The doc reads as a document the whole time, never as a form, never as a console.

Overview

A consulting strategy ships in a document. The operator owns the pen across seven stages — brief, prompt, research, draft, review, signoff, delivered. The client receives the signed document at their own URL with a different inspector — next steps, subscription tiers, transcript, review.

Doc body is shared across the two surfaces. The right inspector swaps per role. No conditional UI inside a shared card; different cards per role.

Stages

1 of 7 · Brief

Operator picks the client, attaches notes, picks depth. Commits the brief.

2 of 7 · Prompt

Agent drafts the research prompt. Operator edits and approves.

3 of 7 · Research

Sources land. Operator skims, prunes, greenlights or asks for more.

4 of 7 · Draft

Agent writes the strategy. Tokens stream into the doc body.

5 of 7 · Review

Operator selects text, opens threads, runs Apply comments, approves replacements.

6 of 7 · Signoff

Operator signs. Doc reflows to delivered shape with stats and signature.

7 of 7 · Delivered, operator

Share URL ready. Inbox holds client comments. Patch path open for typos.

+ Client view

What Sofia opens at the share URL. Next steps, subscription, transcript, review.

Notes

Each stage file mocks one snapshot of the document. Content does not persist between files; opening a stage shows that stage's rest state with plausible mock content.

Stage 5 (Review) carries the full comment-thread mechanic with selection-driven drafts, agent-replied threads marked as Replacement, Approve handler, and reply-with-why refusal on empty submit. Other stages carry static content for visual reference.

The art direction is Document first. Across every stage the book column dominates; the inspector compresses to a stage card and a context card. The signoff shout is the only inverted surface in the book column. Threads are marginalia.
