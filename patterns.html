Atoms
Single-purpose primitives

An atom is the smallest part the kit owns. One token, one utility, one control. Atoms compose into elements and patterns; they do not stand as patterns themselves.

Elements
Atoms composed into single-purpose units

An element binds two or three atoms into one unit with one job. A card with a heading. A field with a label. A stat that pairs a number with a sentence.

.card

Static surface. Transparent at rest, 3% on touch.

.card--tight

Smaller inset. For dense stacks.

.card--shout

Inverted surface. Black ground, white content.

.card--heading

Display title sitting above a group of cards.

.card--interactive

Card with a collapsible body and two CTAs.

.card__heading

Title plus optional caption inside a card.

.field--row

Label on the left, value on the right. One row.

.swatch

Color dot paired with its token name.

.stat

Bold number followed by a one-line fact.
