Atoms
Single-purpose primitives

An atom is the smallest part the kit owns. One token, one utility, one control. Atoms compose into elements and patterns; they do not stand as patterns themselves.

Elements
Atoms composed into single-purpose units

An element binds two or three atoms into one unit with one job. A card with a heading. A field with a label. A stat that pairs a number with a sentence.

Name Description
.card Static surface. Transparent at rest, 3% on touch.
.card--tight Smaller inset. For dense stacks.
.card--shout Inverted surface. Black ground, white content.
.card--heading Display title sitting above a group of cards.
.card--interactive Card with a collapsible body and two CTAs.
.card__heading Title plus optional caption inside a card.
.field--row Label on the left, value on the right. One row.
.swatch Color dot paired with its token name.
.stat Bold number followed by a one-line fact.

Patterns
Reusable compositions with a nameable function

A pattern composes two or more atoms or elements into one reusable shape. Each entry previews a sliced fragment. The small link under each caption opens the slice full size in a new tab.
