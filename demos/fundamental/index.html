Fundamental

Every atom the kit owns, composed the way the kit composes. A document that reads itself.

Seven parts, sidebar on the left, margin on the right. The middle column is what we read.

Prose

Opening
Hero, intro, part break

Commissioner is the only typeface. Regular sits on Medium 500 so the body holds weight on screen. Bold is for headings and the short emphasis that earns it.

The hero runs at 66 / 66. Body at 22 / 32. A 3× jump between the two is what the kit calls radical contrast.

Reading
Body, caption, micro

Paragraphs set in body type. Over three sentences, the paragraph becomes a list or a spec card. This one holds three and stops.

Caption sits at 16 / 24. It is the workhorse inside cards.

Micro is 14 / 20, reserved for metadata and citations.

Lists
Ordered, unordered, inline

An unordered list for items where order carries no weight. A numbered list for steps and rank. Both sit on the same rail.

Motion
Four easings, four durations

Default is 200ms × ease-out on transform and opacity. Reduced motion collapses every duration to 0.01ms.

--ease-out
Hover, focus, state flips.
--ease-spring
Switch thumbs, small confirmations.
--ease-swing
Long reveals, column arrivals.
--ease-in-out
Motion that continues past the frame.

Cards
Static, interactive, shout

Every widget is a card. Transparent at rest, 3% on touch. Three variants, one HTML shape.

Static card

A caption under the title. Raw paragraphs pick up the 12px inset without a card__body wrapper.

Transparent until a pointer enters.

With body wrapper

The card__body wrapper carries the 24px rail when the inner content is not a raw paragraph, list, or spec.

Two lines, one rail.

Tight card. Smaller inset, tighter gap. For dense stacks.

Fields
A row, a label, a value
